FAQ

Is the verified index private or public?

Public. Anyone can search and explore signed entries.

Does the extension prove identity?

No. The MVP uses self-attestation. Identity proofs are planned later.

Why do some sites show as blocked?

Chrome and login pages restrict extensions. Use a normal public page instead.

How do I add to the open feed?

Edit /public/seed.json to add or remove sources.
